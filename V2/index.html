Web & Idenity Design

My name is Francisco Leyva and I am a designer, entrepreneur and strategist. The visual aesthetic experience is an integral part of any brand, business, or project. It conveys a message that connects with the consumers and establishes an identity that is unforgettable. My work involves crafting a design system that embodies a brands unique essence, in which catches the eye. My career as a designer has always been driven by my love for visual aesthetics.

That self-driven passion earned me a post-high-school internship that greatly expanded upon the foundation I had laid and elevated my abilities as well as my understanding of visual aesthetics. For the past five years, I have worked as a graphic designer in the medical marketing industry while simultaneously working on a diverse array of freelance projects.

Web Design Projects

UX/UI Design

Hick’s Law suggest that, users bombarded with choices have to take time to interpret and decide, giving the user work they don’t want. The more stimuli to choose from, the longer it takes to make a decision, in some cases leading them into not making a decision at all.

My focus is always on the user experience. I use a minimalistic approach to ensure that the design has everything it needs and nothing it doesn’t. This approach to design is about the balance of purity and elegance. It has proven to be successful at increasing the conversion rates for the clients I have worked with.
